General Questions

What is Taskly Report PRO?

Taskly Report PRO is a mobile application designed for field professionals to create professional PDF reports with photos, GPS coordinates, voice notes, digital signatures, and more—all from their smartphone.

Who should use Taskly Report PRO?

Taskly Report PRO is perfect for technicians, installers, inspectors, electricians, plumbers, HVAC technicians, furniture installers, property inspectors, moving companies, and any field-based business that needs to generate professional reports.

Is the app free?

Visit the App Store or Google Play for current pricing, availability, and special offers for your region.

Technical Questions

What devices are supported?

Taskly Report PRO is available on iOS and Android devices. Check the App Store or Google Play for specific version requirements for your device.

Do I need an internet connection?

Taskly Report PRO features offline mode, allowing you to create reports without an active internet connection. Your data will sync when you reconnect to the internet.

What file formats are supported?

Taskly Report PRO generates professional PDF reports that can be easily shared, printed, and archived.

Features & Usage

How many photos can I add?

You can add unlimited photos to your reports. The app automatically organizes them by location, time, and content type.

How does the AI assistant work?

The AI assistant learns from your work patterns and automatically creates comprehensive reports from your photos, voice notes, and observations, saving you hours of manual work.

Can clients sign reports?

Yes! Clients can add their digital signatures directly on the report through the app, providing proof of approval.

Is GPS data included in reports?

Yes, Taskly Report PRO automatically captures and includes GPS coordinates, timestamps, and location data in every report for legal documentation and verification.
